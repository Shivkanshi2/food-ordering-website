About Us

Welcome to The Coco Chilli Nestled in the heart of Jharkhand, The Coco Chilli is more than just a café—it's an experience. Inspired by the rustic charm of our forests and the warmth of village life, we bring you a fusion of authentic flavors, fresh ingredients, and a cozy wooden ambience. From hearty local delicacies to global favorites, every dish here is crafted with love, a touch of spice, and a whole lot of soul. Whether you're here for a quick coffee, a leisurely meal, or a celebration with friends, The Coco Chilli is your go-to spot for good food, good vibes, and great memories. Taste the tradition. Feel the comfort. Love the spice.
